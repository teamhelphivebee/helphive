How it works

Simple to join.
Meaningful by design.

From signup to your first match — we've made every step effortless so you can focus on what matters.

01
Join the Waitlist
Tell us if you're a parent or student. Takes 30 seconds. No commitment, no credit card required to get started.
02
Post Your Job
Choose your date, time, whether it's recurring or one-time, and any special notes. Our algorithm does the rest.
03
Get 3 Curated Matches
We show you exactly 3 vetted sitters who are available, nearby, and right for your family. Not a random directory — a curated shortlist.
04
Confirm & Connect
Pick your sitter. They confirm. You arrange payment directly — HelpHive never touches your money. Sitters are independent contractors.
Safety first, always

Your kids deserve
the safest hands.

We built a 4-layer vetting process that no other platform comes close to. Because your family's safety isn't optional.

🔍
Full Background Check
Every sitter passes a comprehensive background check before being listed. No exceptions. No shortcuts. Zero compromise.
🎓
.edu Email Required
University email verification is mandatory. It automatically confirms every sitter is an enrolled, active student — no faking it.
👤
Personal Admin Review
Every application is personally reviewed by our team. We read every profile before a sitter can ever see or accept a job.
⭐
4.0★ Rating Threshold
Sitters below 4.0 stars after 5 jobs are removed from the platform. Full stop. Only the best stay.
